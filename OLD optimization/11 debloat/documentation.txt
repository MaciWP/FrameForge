1 services:

debloating these often will have minimal benefit, its important to only disable ones you 100% don't use, debloating too much of these can brick your system if you dont know what you are doing

you will however see benefit from debloating services if you have a low ram system, or if certain processes are eating up a lot like peripheral softwares



2 debloated apps:

doesn't necessarily improve performance but it will take up less ram



3 device cleaner:

ensure all your currently used devices are plugged in and in use to prevent accidental deletion

old versions of drivers might cause conflicts or slow down hardware detection; this wont increase video game performance



4 background items

stops certain background/startup scripts from running on boot/in the background
