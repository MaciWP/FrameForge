open your registry editor and navigate to this key

Computer\HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\WindowsRuntime\ActivatableClassId\Windows.Gaming.GameBar.PresenceServer.Internal.PresenceWriter

right click on the folder and go to permissions

head to advanced, and change owner

type in your user

then go to add, add yourself once again and give full control, along with admin which should already be pre added

hit apply and ok

you should now be able to change the value ActivationType to 0
